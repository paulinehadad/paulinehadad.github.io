About

Hi there! Welcome to your personal New York City guide. Here, you can enter information about restaurants, bars, clubs, or events that you've been to. Enter the name of the place, how much you liked it, and any extra comment you may have on the place! In each page, you'll see the six most recent pictures posted on Instagram that are related to the specific page you're on.
